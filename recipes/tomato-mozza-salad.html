Tomato Mozzarella Salad

Tomato and mozzarella salad with fresh basil... There's nothing better than a caprese salad, especially in the summer if you can get tomatoes and basil from your garden.

    Ingredients

  • 3 large tomatoes,sliced
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese,sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup minced fresh basil

Directions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients

Step 2

Place tomato slices, alternating with mozzarella slices, on a large serving platter.

Step 3

Combine oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper in a jar with a tight-fitting lid; shake well.

Step 4

Drizzle over tomatoes and mozzarella; sprinkle with basil.

Step 5

Enjoy !
